Dudley Hall

Dudley Hall is a traditional residence hall at Louisiana Tech University, mostly housing first-year students. It offers shared rooms, community bathrooms, and common areas where residents can study, relax, or socialize. Dudley is known for its friendly, community-focused vibe, making it a great place for students who want to build friendships and get involved in campus life.
