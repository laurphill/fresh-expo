Off-Campus living

Moving off campus is something most students prefer to do after their freshman year, as off-campus living offers more freedoms and independence. However, while searching, it may be hard to find a place that is affordable, close to campus, and has the amenities you need. Here you will find a list of the best off-campus apartments that are close to campus and are catered to us students. While apartments may seem expernsive, keep in mind that when you live on campus you have to buy a meal plan which is at least $1,585.

✨The Preserve at Tech

  • A mile from campus
  • 24-hour amenities
  • Gym, pool, computer lab, clubhouse lounge, mailing
  • Free parking
  • 3-bedroom and 4-bedroom apartments
  • Gated
  • Wifi and wired internet
  • Pet friendly
  • In-unit washer and dryer
  • Hosts events for residents often

💰 Cost

  • Offer 10-month, 12-month, and summer leases
  • Private 3-bedroom apartment: Around $700-800 a month
  • Private 4-bedroom apartmment: Around $650-730 a month
