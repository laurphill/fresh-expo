Cottingham Hall

Cottingham Hall is one of Louisiana Tech's traditional residence halls, popular with first-year students. It features shared bedrooms, community bathrooms, and cozy common spaces for studying or socializing. Cottingham has a strong sense of community and often hosts events to help residents connect and make the most of their college experience.
