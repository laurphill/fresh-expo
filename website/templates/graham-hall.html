Graham Hall

Graham Hall is one of Louisiana Tech's traditional residence halls that offers a classic college living experience. Known for its strong sense of community, Graham is a place where many first-year students form lasting friendships. The building has shared bedrooms and community bathrooms, with common areas that often become social spots for residents. Graham is a great fit for students who want to stay active on campus and enjoy a lively, supportive environment.
