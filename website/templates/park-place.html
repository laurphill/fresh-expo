Park Place Apartments

Park Place Apartments are made up of the Jenkins, McFarland, and Hutcheson Commons. Park Place offers the independence of off-campus living while still being close enough to easily access classes and campus activities. It's a popular choice for students looking for a quieter, more home-like environment.
