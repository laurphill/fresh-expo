🐾 What is PawPoint?

PawPoint is a student-powered platform designed to help Louisiana Tech students find their path on campus. From discovering clubs and events to joining study groups and staying on top of deadlines, PawPoint makes it easy to connect, collaborate, and thrive throughout your college journey.
