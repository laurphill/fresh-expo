PawPoint is a student-driven platform created to help new Louisiana Tech students transition smoothly into college life. It provides curated resources, helpful guides, group chats, and organizational tools to stay on top of deadlines and stay connected. Our goal with Paw Point is to lessen the stress for new students entering into a college environment by centralizing all of the most important information we wished we had easier access to as incoming freshmen.
