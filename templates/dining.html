Tech Table is an All-You-Care-to-Eat residential dining hall located in the Student Center at Louisiana Tech University. It offers freshly prepared meals including homestyle dishes, international options, and chef-inspired specials. Students can enjoy a variety of healthy, customizable menu choices. The casual setting features TVs, music, and lounge-style seating. Tech Table also hosts special events with themed meals and provides allergen-friendly options through the True Balance station.
